Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about CompareVision Pro

What types of images can CompareVision Pro analyze?
CompareVision Pro supports over 50 image formats including JPG, PNG, WebP, BMP, GIF, TIFF, and RAW formats. It's optimized for static object comparison and works best with high-resolution images of products, components, documents, and other non-moving subjects.
How accurate is the comparison algorithm?
Our advanced algorithms achieve 99.9% accuracy in controlled conditions. The system uses multiple comparison metrics including pixel-level analysis, structural similarity, and feature matching to provide comprehensive results. Accuracy may vary based on image quality and lighting conditions.
Is my data secure?
Absolutely. All image processing happens locally in your browser. Your images are never uploaded to our servers, ensuring complete privacy and security. This client-side processing also means you can use CompareVision Pro offline once the page is loaded.
Can I integrate CompareVision Pro into my existing workflow?
Yes! CompareVision Pro offers flexible integration options including REST API, Python SDK, and JavaScript libraries. We also support batch processing and can integrate with popular tools like Zapier, Make (formerly Integromat), and custom enterprise systems.
What makes this different from facial recognition software?
CompareVision Pro is specifically designed for static object comparison, not facial recognition. Our algorithms are optimized for detecting differences in products, components, documents, and other non-human subjects. This specialization allows us to achieve higher accuracy for industrial and commercial applications.
