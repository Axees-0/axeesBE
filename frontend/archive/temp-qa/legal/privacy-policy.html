Cookies and Tracking Technologies

What are Cookies?
 Cookies are small files stored on your device to help us provide a more customized experience. We use both temporary (session) and persistent cookies.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for core site functionality, like account login and loading content properly.
  • Functional Cookies: Store your preferences to personalize your experience.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand user behavior and improve site performance.
  • Advertising Cookies: Track browsing behavior to display more relevant ads.
  • Social Media Cookies: Enable sharing and interaction with third-party social platforms.

Managing Cookies
 Most browsers allow you to control or disable cookies. However, disabling them may affect your user experience.
